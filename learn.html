The Learning Center will help walk you through the day-to-day experience of using and understanding KISSmetrics. In a nutshell, these are the five major steps your team will be repeating as you use KISSmetrics (or any other analytics platform for that matter).

The Analytics Cycle

  1. Decide on which reports you currently need.
  2. Assess what new pieces of data you will need to send to KISSmetrics, in the form of events and properties.
  3. Choose the method(s) you will be sending data, based on the developer resources available.
  4. Implement the event/property tracking and test.
  5. Create and run the reports you planned for.
